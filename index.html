About

I am a web designer and developer with several years of experience working for a variety of nonprofits and small businesses. With a background in fine art and creative writing, I bring a creative perspective and editorial eye to my work. I am passionate about technology and learning as much as possible in this ever-changing industry.

When I’m not coding and developing websites, I spend my time making and marketing my collage and illustration artwork. I make collages entirely from magazine strips with a process I call “painting with paper."
